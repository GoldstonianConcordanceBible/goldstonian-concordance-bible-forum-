# Contributing

## Allowed contributions
- Propose new controlled vocabulary terms (with definitions)
- Suggest crosswalk relations (with reasoning)
- Add discussion notes and questions

## Disallowed contributions
- Secrets or private data
- Malware, obfuscated code, or unsafe scripts
- Claims of “official access” to private systems

## Process
1) Open a Discussion for the idea
2) If it’s worth promoting into the canonical library, propose it as a structured change with references
3) Maintain humility and avoid turning the agent into an authority over Scripture
